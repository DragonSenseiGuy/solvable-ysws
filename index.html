What is Solvable?

Solvable YSWS is a You Ship, We Ship program. Your task is to find a problem or inconvenience in your life and 3D design a solution. Whether it's a small tool or a simple design, it's a solution built by you!

Design it, submit it, and we'll 3D print it and ship it straight to your door.

How It Works

1

Find a Problem

Look for an inconvenience in your life that needs a fix.

2

Design in CAD

Model your solution in your favorite 3D software.

3

Submit Form

Tell us about your project and request hardware.

4

Get It Shipped

Receive your part printed and ready to use!

Requirements

Eligibility

  • Be 13-18 years old
  • Be a member of the Hack Club Slack
  • Submit your own original work

Project Requirements

  • Solve a real, specific problem
  • Be something you can physically build
  • Be shippable within the timeframe
  • Include documentation of your process
